Mark Zuckerberg

Mark Zuckerberg is a visionary technology entrepreneur, born on May 14, 1984, in White Plains, New York. As the co-founder and CEO of Facebook, he has been instrumental in revolutionizing global communication through the creation and expansion of the world's largest social networking platform since its inception in 2004. Under his leadership, Facebook has evolved into a powerhouse in the tech industry, with strategic acquisitions like Instagram and WhatsApp. Zuckerberg's innovative contributions extend beyond business, as demonstrated by the Chan Zuckerberg Initiative, founded in 2015 with his wife Priscilla Chan. This philanthropic endeavor reflects his dedication to addressing global challenges, with a focus on initiatives in education, scientific research, and healthcare. Mark Zuckerberg's portfolio showcases not only his entrepreneurial prowess but also his commitment to leveraging technology for positive societal impact.
